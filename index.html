The Effects of the 2010 Citizens United Decision

To save everyone a bit of time, the result of the Citizens United decision is one of the largest blows to the
integrity of American democracy in the last decade. It removed the already faltering legal
restraints on megacorporations acting purely for their own benefit, and works in variance with the legal basis it was argued over.
